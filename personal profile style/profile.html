John Doe

John Doe is a placeholder name commonly used in English-speaking countries to represent an anonymous or unspecified person. This name is often employed in legal contexts, example scenarios, and documentation where a real name is not known or needs to be kept confidential. "John Doe" is used for men, while "Jane Doe" is the equivalent for women.
